🔒 Security

Military-grade security for your files

We use the strongest encryption algorithms to protect your data. With zero-knowledge architecture, your privacy is guaranteed — not even our team can access your files.

🔐

Twofish Encryption

Client-side encryption using the Twofish algorithm with 256-bit keys — one of the most secure ciphers available.

🌐

TLS/SSL in Transit

All data transfers are protected with TLS/SSL encryption, ensuring your files are safe during upload and download.

🏢

Secure Data Centers

Files stored in Tier IV data centers with 24/7 physical security, biometric access, and redundant power systems.

🚫

Zero-Knowledge Privacy

We never store your encryption keys or password. Your data remains completely private and inaccessible to anyone but you.
